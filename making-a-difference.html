Making A Difference At Olsen Day, we believe in making a difference for our clients, and we are pleased to participate with and support the following organizations that make a difference in the lives of others. The China Resource Center serves the rapid growth of the church in China through rural bible distribution, rural church construction and engaging Westerners in the effort. Family Legacy Missions International fosters a dynamic and growing relationship between the families of America and the orphaned and vulnerable children of Zambia. The Tent reaches and teaches women in Israel. Campus Renewal Ministries is bringing transformation to The University of Texas. The Cy-Fair ISD Mentoring Program links adult mentors with students to encourage them to finish school and to focus on future education, career and life goals.
